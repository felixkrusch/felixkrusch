<p>2026: Your website shouldn't fight you every time you want to update it.</p>

<p>I actually built a proper multi-language blog in WordPress (EN, DE, FR). Started posting and immediately hit the wall. Even today there's still no real native multi-language support. Every single SEO field, schema markup, and meta tag has to be filled out manually.</p>

<p>That's exactly when it clicked. As a developer, simply using chat as the CMS is dramatically more efficient.</p>

<p>For non-technical clients, we go one better: AI agents that act as your full website department.</p>

<p>The workflow is stupidly simple.</p>

<p>Send the agent an email with a blog post draft or just an idea. They refine it, handle all the technical details, and send you a staging link for review. You approve and they publish.</p>

<p>Need to change the phone number in the footer? Or swap an image on any page? Same thing. The agent handles it, sends you a staging link, and publishes only after your green light.</p>

<p>You stay in full control the entire time. No lost access. No surprises.</p>

<p>No more Gutenberg wrestling. No plugin hell. No manual SEO drudgery.</p>

<p>Just fast, clean results.</p>

<p>This isn't agents writing everything for you (though they could). It's agents sitting between you and the site so you never have to touch the backend again.</p>

<p>Simpler really is better.</p>
